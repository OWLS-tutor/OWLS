What I Offer

Private UCT graduated English and Afrikaans tutor. Panorama primary school is within walking distance from where I live.

About My Lessons

My goal is to involve the students in the class as much as possible and encourage their active participation. The teacher should always involve the students by continuously asking them questions. I believe that each lesson should be tailored to each student's needs and focused on helping them achieve their goals.

About Me

My name is Werner and I live in Cape Town, South Africa. I have adored everything about the use of language for as long as I can remember. Because of my love for language, I majored in English and Afrikaans at the University of Cape Town. I also did my honors in library science because of my love for books. After university, I decided to share my love for language with new students. Consequently, I got a TEFL (Teaching English as a Foreign Language) level 5 advanced diploma. Teaching is my passion and I hope to see you soon, as my new students soon.
